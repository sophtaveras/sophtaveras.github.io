A little bit about me

Hello, I'm Sophia

I am a designer with experience in web and graphic design. I grew up and currently live in South Florida and graduated from the Art Institute of Fort lauderdale years ago for design.

For me, design is not just about selling a product and nothing more; its about providing a memorable experience for people that will leave a good and lasting impression of a product, which will encourage them to keep coming back.

I specialize in designing products across multiple mediums such as: mobile, web, and print.

I like problem solving, research, and design. I love slow ethical fashion, skincare, musical theatre, and being creative.
